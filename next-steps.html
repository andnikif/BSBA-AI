Next Steps & Review

Proposed actions for moving forward with the BS-BAIA program proposal.

Suggested Next Steps

To advance the development and approval of the BS-BAIA program, the following steps are recommended:

  • Initial Review & Feedback: Gather feedback on this proposal from key stakeholders, including faculty and relevant committees.
  • Curriculum Committee Review: Formal submission of the program structure and course details to the School of Management curriculum committee.
  • Resource Assessment: Detailed evaluation of faculty expertise requirements, necessary software/technology resources, and potential budget implications.
  • University Approval Process: Navigating the formal university channels for new program approval.
  • Marketing & Recruitment Plan: Developing a strategy to promote the new program to prospective students.

Key Considerations

As the proposal moves forward, key areas for consideration include:

  • Faculty Development: Ensuring faculty possess the necessary expertise in applied AI or identifying needs for training/hiring.
  • Technology & Software: Access to relevant analytics platforms, AI tools (potentially cloud-based), and Python environments for student use.
  • Experiential Learning Integration: Finalizing partnerships or projects for the capstone course and potential internships.
  • Cross-Disciplinary Collaboration: Opportunities for collaboration with other NYIT departments (e.g., Engineering and Computing Sciences for guest lectures or workshops).
